NOVAR ADVERTISING POLICY

1. General Advertising Standards

  • By submitting an advertisement for placement on NOVAR, the Advertiser agrees to abide by the following policies
  • Ads must align with NOVAR’s educational values and must not harm, mislead, or exploit users
  • NOVAR reserves the right to approve, reject, remove, or modify any advertisement at its sole discretion
  • Ads must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards
  • Ads cannot misrepresent products, services, or endorsements in any way

2. Content Restrictions

  • The following content is strictly prohibited:
    • False or misleading claims, including deceptive pricing
    • Promotion of illegal activities, violence, hate speech, or discrimination
    • Explicit, sexual, or inappropriate content
    • Content that negatively impacts learners, parents, or educators
    • Any direct competition with NOVAR without prior approval

3. Technical & Format Requirements

  • All ads must be submitted in the required format and dimensions
  • NOVAR may reject ads that are blurry, low-quality, or improperly formatted
  • Advertisers are responsible for ensuring that their ads function correctly

4. No Guarantee of Results

  • NOVAR does not guarantee any impressions, clicks, or conversions
  • Advertisers acknowledge that ad performance depends on multiple factors beyond NOVAR’s control

5. Payment & No-Refund Policy

  • All payments must be made upfront before ad placement
  • Strictly No Refunds: Once payment is processed, no refunds will be issued under any circumstances, including but not limited to:
    • Performance dissatisfaction
    • Campaign cancellation by the Advertiser
    • Technical issues from the Advertiser’s end
    • Removal due to policy violations

6. Ad Removal & Suspension

  • NOVAR reserves the right to suspend or remove any ad if it violates policies, receives complaints, or damages NOVAR’s reputation
  • No refunds will be given if an ad is removed due to a policy violation

7. Advertiser Responsibilities

  • Ensure ad content is accurate, legal, and owned by the Advertiser
  • Ensure that ads do not infringe on copyrights, trademarks, or other legal protections
  • Be accountable for any claims, lawsuits, or liabilities arising from the ad

8. Data Collection & Tracking

  • Advertisers may use tracking pixels or analytics tools only with NOVAR’s approval
  • Advertisers cannot collect personal data from NOVAR users without explicit consent

9. Changes to Policy

  • NOVAR reserves the right to update these policies at any time with a 30-day notice

NOVAR ADVERTISING PRIVACY POLICY

1. Data Collected

  • NOVAR collects the following from Advertisers:
    • Business name, contact details, and payment information
    • Ad content and campaign details
    • Analytics on ad performance (views, clicks, impressions)

2. How Data Is Used

  • To process ad placements and payments
  • To monitor ad performance and compliance with NOVAR policies
  • To communicate updates, renewals, and policy changes

3. Data Sharing & Protection

  • NOVAR does not sell or share advertiser data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Data is securely stored and protected against unauthorized access

4. Advertiser Responsibilities

  • Advertisers must comply with data protection laws and ensure that their ads do not collect personal data unlawfully
  • Any third-party tracking tools used must be pre-approved by NOVAR

5. Policy Updates

  • NOVAR may update this Privacy Policy at any time with a 30-day notice

Advertiser Agreement Confirmation

By submitting an advertising request, the Advertiser confirms they have read, understood, and agreed to NOVAR’s Advertising Policy and Privacy Policy
